Taken

The best 1st person shooting game is the entire universe

I’m an alien whose starship crashed on Earth and I was adopted into a human family, where I met my earth sister. Along with me, a few aliens from my home planet also escaped aboard my starship. From that day my mission was to safeguard earth from the crook aliens who harm peace of the society.

Five of the big bad bosses decide to unite and remove the final obstacle they have been facing so far and…….. I get abducted and I suddenly woke up in a forest full of aliens who were preventing my escape. I kill through all of them to escape and at the end of level 3 I found an old friend from my home planet fighting the boss and before dying, he tells us that they have my sister too and I should save her as soon as we can.I continue the journey to hunt the remaining bosses and rescue my sister.
